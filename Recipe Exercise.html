Recipe for Ground Beef Taco

Quick and easy recipe that you will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Tortillas
  • shredded Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the ground meat and onion saut√© for 5 minutes and break it up with the back of a wooden spoon.
  2. Add all the taco seasoning and stir to combine. Continue to cook the ground meat and break it down with the back of a wooden spoon until it's fully cooked and no pink remains.
  3. Once cooked, add 2 tablespoons of water and reduce to a simmer. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve the taco meat with tortillas, shredded cheese, salsa, and any other taco fixings you prefer. Voila, Ground Beef Tacos are done!
